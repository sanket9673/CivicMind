Justice: Social, Economic, and Political

Theory : Justice is essential to ensure that individuals are treated fairly and equally. Whether it’s in the workplace, educational settings, or in front of the law, the concept of justice ensures that everyone gets what they are entitled to, without discrimination or bias. In daily life, justice protects individuals from exploitation and unfair treatment.

• Article 14: Guarantees "Equality before the law" and "Equal protection of the law." It ensures that every citizen gets fair treatment under the law.
• Article 21: Protects the right to life and personal liberty. This includes living with dignity and being treated fairly in any circumstance.



Example : If your employer underpays you, Article 14 can be invoked to ensure that you receive equal pay for equal work. This is supported by the Preamble's guarantee of justice.
